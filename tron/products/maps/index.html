FAQs

How do I get directions?

Enter your starting point and destination, select your route, and follow the step-by-step directions.

Can I change my route?

Yes, you can adjust your route anytime, and the app will update directions automatically.

Is this a Maps App?

No, the Maps and Directions product is a widget within aiBrowser. When you download, you'll get the full browser along with other productivity tools.

Are walking directions available?

Yes, walking directions are available with clear, turn-by-turn steps to guide you safely and efficiently to your destination.
